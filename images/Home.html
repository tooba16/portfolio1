Governor Kamran Tessori Launches Groundbreaking Artificial Intelligence and Computing Program GIAIC


In an effort to drive technological advancement and empower youth with future-ready skills, Governor Kamran Tessori has launched an ambitious Artificial Intelligence and Computing Program. This initiative reflects the Governor’s vision of transforming the province into a hub of innovation and technological excellence, with a strong emphasis on preparing young minds for the digital age.
The AI and Computing Program is designed to equip students, professionals, and aspiring entrepreneurs with cutting-edge skills in artificial intelligence, data science, machine learning, and other emerging technologies. By fostering an ecosystem of knowledge and innovation, the program aims to create a workforce that can thrive in the global tech landscape and bring transformative solutions to local challenges.
Governor Kamran Tessori stated, “The future belongs to those who are equipped with the right tools to innovate and lead. This initiative is not only an investment in technology but an investment in our youth, in our future. I am committed to ensuring that every young person in our province has the opportunity to learn, grow, and excel in fields that are shaping the world.”
Under this program, a range of activities, including workshops, certifications, and hands-on projects, will be organized. The curriculum is tailored to make complex topics accessible to beginners while offering in-depth insights to those already in the field. Special scholarships and grants will be available to ensure inclusive participation, enabling talented individuals from all backgrounds to join this transformative journey.
The initiative has also garnered support from leading tech companies, universities, and AI experts, who have partnered with the program to offer mentorship, resources, and industry insight. This collaborative approach will bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring that graduates of the program are ready to tackle real-world challenges.
Governor Tessori’s vision for an AI-powered future is one where technology enhances lives, strengthens the economy, and unlocks the full potential of our society. Through this initiative, the province is on the path to becoming a leader in technology and innovation, creating new opportunities for generations to come.
For more information on enrollment and program details, please visit the official website or contact the Governor’s office.
